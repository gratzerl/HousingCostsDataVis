Development of housing costs

This data visualization project tries to highlight how the share of housing costs has developed over the last 10 years. All data was taken from eurostat.

Most important terms

How to read the charts

The visualtization can be split into two parts: the ciruclar bar charts and a bubble chart.

The data in the explanation charts is not real.

Circular bar chart

For each European country a ciruclar bar chart displays the share of the housing costs of the country's average disposable income for the years from 2011-2019.

When hovering over a bar the percentage and the year are displayed.

Clicking on a bar displays how the housing costs for this year are made up.
